Our Mission

We are a student run non-for-profit with the main goal being to enhance the welfare of seniors in retirement homes by bringing in puppies for an afternoon full of fun and community connection!

 
 
 

    Please feel free to reach out if you would like to organize a Puppies4seniors event for your retirement home!
